-Recipe-----------------------------------------------------
| Sauteed Zucchini and Peas                                |
-Ingredients------------------------------------------------
| 2 Zucchini (quartered 1/2" slices)                       |
| 2 oz. Peas                                               |
| 0.3 oz. Butter                                           |
| 1 oz. Parmesan                                           |
-Cooking-Instructions---------------------------------------
| Medium non-stick pan over medium high heat, add 2t oil.  |
| Saute Zucchini 2-3 mins undisturbed. Stir in Salt 2min.  |
| Add Peas, butter, 1t. water. Stir 1-2minutes.            |
