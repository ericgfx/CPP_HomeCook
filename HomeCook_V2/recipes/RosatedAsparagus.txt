-Recipe-----------------------------------------------------
| Crispy Onion Asparagus Spears w Vinagrette               |
-Ingredients------------------------------------------------
| 12 oz. Asparagus                                         |
| 1 Lemon (zested, 1/2 juiced)                             |
| 0.5 oz. Crispy Fried Onions                              |
| 0.25 oz. Red Pepper Flakes                               |
-Cooking-Instructions---------------------------------------
| Preheat oven 400. Cover baking sheet with foil and spray |
| Trim ends off asparagus. Massage with oil and S&P.       |
| Spread into a single layer. Roast 10-12 minutes.         |
| Top with vinagrette and crispy onions.                   |
-Sauce------------------------------------------------------
| combine 1t. lemon zest, 1T. juice, 1t. olive oil, flakes |
| to taste, and pinch of salt.                             |
