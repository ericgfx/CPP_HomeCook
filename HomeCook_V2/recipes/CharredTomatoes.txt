-Recipe-----------------------------------------------------
|Charred Tomatoes                                          |
-Ingredients------------------------------------------------
|4oz. Grape Tomatoes                                       |
-Cooking-Instructions---------------------------------------
|Bring Pan to high heat                                    |
|Slice tomatoes                                            |
|Add 1tsp oil and tomatoes                                 |
|Stir until lightly charred 2min                           |
|Salt and serve.                                           |
