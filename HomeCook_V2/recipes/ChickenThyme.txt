-Recipe-----------------------------------------------------
| Chicken with Pesto Thyme Crema                           |
-Ingredients------------------------------------------------
| 2 oz Sour Cream                                          |
| 3 Thyme Sprigs (minced)                                  |
| 2 Tbsp. Basil Pesto                                      |
| 13 oz Boneless Skinless Chicken Breast                   |
-Cooking-Instructions---------------------------------------
| Pat Chicken Breasts dry, season with S&P                 |
| Medium sized non-stick pan on medium-high heat           |
| add 2t. olive oil, add chicken 5-7 min./side             |
| while chicken cooks make sauce                           |
-Sauce------------------------------------------------------
| mix thyme, pesto, sour cream, 1t oil and pinch salt      |
| set aside                                                |
------------------------------------------------------------
