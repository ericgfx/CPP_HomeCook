-Recipe-----------------------------------------------------
|Chicken with Poblano Cream Sauce                          |
-Ingredients------------------------------------------------
|13oz Chicken Breasts                                      |
|1 Poblano Pepper (finely chopped)                         |
|2 garlic cloves minced                                    |
|4oz Light Cream                                           |
-Cooking-Instructions---------------------------------------
|Saute Chicken Breasts in medium hot saucepan.             |
|5-7min per side. Remove Chicken, tent with foil.          |
-Sauce------------------------------------------------------
|Add garlic and Poblano to medium hot pan.                 |
|Stir until soft, 1-2 minutes.                             |
|Add Cream and pinch of Salt.                              |
|Stir constantly until thick 1-2 minutes                   |
------------------------------------------------------------
