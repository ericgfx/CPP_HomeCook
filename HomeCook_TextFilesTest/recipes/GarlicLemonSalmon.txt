-Recipe-----------------------------------------------------
|Garlic And Lemon Crusted Salmon                           |
-Ingredients------------------------------------------------
|2 Cloves Garlic                                           |
|1 Lemon                                                   |
|12 oz Salmon Fillets                                      |
-Cooking-Instructions---------------------------------------
|Dry Salmon, season with salt\nSaute thinly sliced garlic  |
|10 min medium heat, remove, drain.                        |
|Saute Salmon in 1tsp oil, 4-6 min. per side med-high heat.|
|Plate salmon with garlic chips and lemon zest.            |
